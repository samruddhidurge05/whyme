Pretty Explained 📸

Periods happen when the lining of the uterus sheds, which causes bleeding through the vagina. This is part of the menstrual cycle and usually happens every month. It’s your body’s natural way of preparing for pregnancy, and if no pregnancy happens, it resets itself. It can come with cramps, mood swings, and cravings, but it’s also totally normal and healthy 🌸✨
